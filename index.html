Data Literacy Series

You are here

Home / Research Data Services

A monthly series of infographics on research data

Sign up for our Data Literacy Series and receive the infographics straight to your inbox!

This series was officially launched in August 2020. It aims to complement the department's instructional efforts and mission to promote data literacy and data management practices. The DLS handouts are distributed under the CC BY-NC-SA 4.0license (learn more). If you’d like to suggest a topic for an upcoming issue, please submit your feedback to rds@library.ucsb.edu﻿.

You may navigate the handouts gallery,chronologically, or browse the series by topics (bottom of this page). Click on the thumbnail to view and download the PDF.
